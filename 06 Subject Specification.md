# Optional Subject Specification Guide

This is a user-facing guide, not a blank form that must be appended to every image prompt.

A complete request may contain only:

```text
[alternate form], [finish]
```

Example:

```text
Japanese spider crab, vintage
```

The agent treats the user's entire request as the completed Subject Specification. Never require optional fields and never ask for details that were not supplied.

Optional details may be written naturally:

- Personality or role
- Primary and secondary colors
- Patterns, markings, decals, or insignia
- Weapon preference, including no weapon
- Special notes
- Required transformation relationships
- Pose or presentation override

Example with optional details:

```text
Japanese spider crab, vintage, burnt orange and ivory, patient deep-sea sentry, long rifle
```
